Earn Economy

Earn for your contributions

BLVD tokens are minted as users of the ecosystem make contributions. The earnings are openly baked into the BLVD erc-20 token smart contract.

Current mapping ecosystems leverage your data without providing a direct economic benefit to their end users. BLVD enables a token economy that grows with the community generating its value.

Community Maintained

Decentralized Control

The BLVD token will be managed via a DAO on the Ethereum Blockchain. The BLVD smart contract has been written to enable the community to adjust contribution earnings over time, allowing token holders to optimize the economics over time.
