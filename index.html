Drive

Simply install the BULVRD Drive app and start driving! Featuring real time road reports like traffic, police, and hazards. Explore the world via futuristic 3d maps, Augmented Reality, and Apple CarPlay. Traffic aware turn by turn navigation will get you to your destination on time!

Earn

By simply using BULVRD while driving you will begin to earn tokens. Make road reports like traffic and hazards to earn more. Use our Augmented Reality experience for even higher earnings.

Use

The BULVRD token is ERC-20 compliant, meaning out of the box support for nearly every cryptocurrency exchange. Use your tokens by dropping on your routes to work to help encourage reporting, helping you find the quickest way. Stake your tokens to place NFTs (like Cryptokitties) on the map. Or run a location based ad on the BULVRD map.

The Ecosystem

Ethereum

BULVRD leverages the Ethereum blockchain with Smart Contracts to openly manage rewards, and create new data sets via IPFS. Meaning global availability without location or platform limitations.

3D Maps

Ground up custom styled 3d mapping experience puts the focus on roads, traffic, and route reports. Real time updates, traffic aware navigation.

Augmented Reality

All new geospatial Augmented Reality experiences give community members an all new way to interact with the world around them, including automation via Machine Learning and AI.

Hyper Accurate

Our GPS/location algorithms enable 1-3m accuracy, meaning lane level detection. This helps us let you know when you're in the wrong lane for your next navigation turn, and make community reports more accurate.

Digital Collectibles

We are giving non-fungible tokens (digital collectibles) an all new use. You can stake your digital collectible on the map to show the world, or use it as a new marketing avenue when selling it. You will also be able to earn custom BULVRD digital collectibles for contributions in the ecosystem.

Visibility

The BULVRD reward feed is posted to IPFS building transparency into the ecosystem. The token economics are baked into the Smart Contract, as well as the state for each address earnings. Making it possible to audit at any time. Enabling a fully transparent economy.

BLVD Token

BLVD is an ERC-20 smart contract token that is central to the earning economics of BULVRD.


BULVRD IQ

BULVRD IQ is an all new mapping experience. Melding GPS, Camera Vision, and Machine Learning, we are creating an all new street level visual experience. Helping you identify exactly where a hazard report is, or where your next navigation turn is.


BULVRD U L T R A

BULVRD Ultra is a monthly subscription that lives on the Ethereum blockchain via smart contract! BULVRD Ultra enables extended features and functionality to subscribers. 1250 BLVD tokens per month, cancel at anytime!


BLVD Map Styles

Limited edition digital collectible map styles enable you to customize your BULVRD experience. Each map style is a non-fungible token meaning you can buy / sell / trade app customization!


BLVD HODLr

BULVRD Drive incentivizes keeping BLVD tokens in your connected Ethereum Wallet by automatically unlocking in-app functions and features.


0xEarth Support

BULVRD supports 0xEarth, the digital land ownership standard of earth. Each LAND token represents a unique location of earth. This LAND enables only the owner to interact with that location. In BULVRD Drive, this will provide backing to our BULVRD Local advertising platform.


Digital Collectibles

BULVRD gives your digital collectibles an all new use. You can set your digital collectible as your map icon show to other as you drive around. Or you can stake BLVD tokens to place your digital collectible on the map, anywhere on earth.


BULVRD Events

BULVRD will feature special events that can be hosted by anyone. BULVRD Events can be community driving events on set routes, scavenger hunts for digital collectibles, or just a group drive with friends.


Spotify

BULVRD Drive integrates directly with Spotify for easy control and visuals of your music.


Advance GPS

BULVRD has built custom GPS / GNSS algorithms to provide lane level location accuracy on select Android devices, with AR cloud points helping provide further accuracy improvements..


BULVRD Dashboard

The BULVRD dashboard is a streamlined dashboard system to track and use your BLVD tokens and earnings. Featuring integrations with Metamask, 0x, OpenSea, Coinbase, and Wyre for simple transactions.


BULVRD Local

BULVRD Local gives businesses and brands a new hyper local focused path to spreading awareness. BULVRD Local is powered by the BLVD token, and passes certain earnings on to users.


Vehicle Intelligence

Connect BULVRD Drive directly with your vehicle (1996 and Newer) to enable direct vehicle intelligence, improved mapping and navigation experience, and increased BLVD token earnings.


Token Drops

BULVRD enables dropping tokens along a route to open a new means of incentivising driving and providing reports. A business can drop tokens infront of their stores, or a user can drop tokens on various routes to work to find the quickest way!
