Drive and Earn

BULVRD is the global community mapping and route reporting ecosystem that allows you to earn cryptocurrency tokens while you drive. Drive and explore the world via 3D maps, Augmented Reality, and in-car with Apple CarPlay.

Features

Go Drive

Drive around the world - report traffic, accidents, and more - Turn by turn navigation - 3D Maps and Augmented Reality

You Earn

Earn BULVRD tokens by driving, making reports, and contributing to the community

Then Use

Use your tokens by gifting to other users as 'thanks', 'airdrop' on routes to encourage others to drive, buy digital assets, subscriptions, and more.

Ethereum

BULVRD leverages the Ethereum blockchain with Smart Contracts to openly manage rewards, and create new data sets via IPFS.

Augmented Reality

All new geospatial Augmented Reality experiences give community memebers an all new way to interact with the world around them, including automation via Machine Learning.

Global

BULVRD is available globally without location or platform limitations.
